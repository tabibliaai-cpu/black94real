By accessing or using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

We understand that when you use our platform, you trust us with your personal information. We take that trust seriously and strive to provide a transparent, secure, and privacy-respecting experience for all of our users. This policy applies to all users of the Black94 platform, including registered members, visitors, and any other individuals who interact with our Services.

We encourage you to read this Privacy Policy carefully and to check this page periodically for any changes. In this policy, "personal information" means any information that can be used to identify you directly or indirectly, alone or in combination with other information. We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the platform, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our Services, or otherwise contact us. We also automatically collect certain information when you visit, use, or navigate our Services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our Services for you. The types of information we collect depend on how you interact with our platform and the choices you make. Below is a detailed description of the categories of information we may collect:

Personal Information: When you create a Black94 account, we collect personal identification information such as your full name, username, email address, phone number, and profile picture. You may also choose to provide additional information in your profile, including a bio, date of birth, gender, location, website links, and other details you wish to share with the community. If you use social sign-on (such as Google, Apple, or Facebook authentication), we may receive authentication tokens and basic profile information from those services. We also collect information you provide when you communicate with us, such as when you submit support requests, report issues, or participate in surveys.

Usage Data: We automatically collect information about your interactions with our Services, including your browsing patterns, features used, search queries, content viewed, likes, shares, comments, messages sent and received, accounts you follow, and other engagement metrics. We track session duration, frequency of use, and navigation paths through the application. This usage data helps us understand how users interact with our platform so we can improve the user experience and develop new features that our community finds valuable.

Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access our Services, including the device type, model, manufacturer, operating system and version, browser type and version, screen resolution, unique device identifiers (such as advertising identifiers, IMEI, or device serial number), mobile network information, carrier, and other technical specifications. This information is used for device optimization, troubleshooting, analytics, and security purposes.

Location Information: With your explicit consent, we may collect and process information about your precise or approximate geographic location. This may include GPS coordinates, Wi-Fi access points, Bluetooth beacons, cell tower data, and IP-based location data. We use location information to provide location-based features such as geotagged posts, local content discovery, nearby user suggestions, and targeted recommendations. You can control location permissions through your device settings at any time. If you decline to share location data, some features may not be available or may provide a degraded experience.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We and our third-party service providers use cookies, web beacons, pixels, device fingerprints, local storage, and similar tracking technologies to collect and store information about your interactions with our Services. These technologies help us remember your preferences, analyze traffic, deliver personalized content, measure advertising effectiveness, and provide enhanced functionality. We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device until they expire or you delete them). You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings, though disabling cookies may affect certain features of our Services.

Important: We do not sell your personal information to any third parties. We collect only what is necessary to provide, improve, and secure our Services, and we are transparent about how that information is used.

5. Data Storage & Security

The security and integrity of your personal information is a top priority for Black94. We implement robust technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Below is an overview of our data storage and security practices:

Data Storage: Your personal information is stored on secure servers operated by Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and Firebase, our trusted cloud infrastructure providers. Data is stored in data centers located in the United States and may be replicated to backup facilities for redundancy and disaster recovery purposes. Our servers are housed in facilities with stringent physical security controls, including 24/7 monitoring, biometric access controls, surveillance systems, and environmental controls. By using industry-leading cloud providers, we benefit from their extensive security certifications and compliance programs.

Encryption: We employ strong encryption measures to protect your data both in transit and at rest. All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS (Transport Layer Security) 1.2 or higher with strong cipher suites. Data stored on our servers is encrypted using AES-256 encryption or equivalent standards. Sensitive information such as passwords is hashed and salted using industry-standard algorithms (bcrypt or Argon2) and is never stored in plaintext. We continuously evaluate and update our encryption standards to incorporate the latest security best practices and technological advances.

Access Controls: We implement strict access controls to limit who can access your personal information within our organization. Access to user data is granted on a need-to-know basis and is limited to employees, contractors, and agents who require such access to perform their job functions. All personnel with data access undergo background checks, receive comprehensive security training, and are bound by confidentiality obligations. We maintain detailed audit logs of data access to monitor for unauthorized or inappropriate access and to support security investigations.

Security Monitoring and Incident Response: We maintain comprehensive security monitoring systems that continuously scan for threats, vulnerabilities, and suspicious activity. We conduct regular security assessments, penetration testing, and vulnerability scans to identify and address potential weaknesses in our systems. In the event of a data security incident or breach, we have an incident response plan in place to quickly contain, investigate, and remediate the issue. We will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by applicable data breach notification laws, and we will provide timely and transparent communication about the incident and the steps taken to address it.

Data Retention: We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our Services. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information from our active records within a reasonable timeframe, except where we are required or permitted to retain certain information to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, protect against fraud, or for legitimate business purposes such as analytics and record-keeping. Anonymized or aggregated data that cannot reasonably be used to identify you may be retained indefinitely.

6. Your Rights

We believe you should have meaningful control over your personal information. Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the following rights with respect to your personal information. We are committed to honoring these rights and facilitating your requests in a timely manner:

Right of Access: You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you. Upon verification of your identity, we will provide you with a copy of your personal data in a commonly used, machine-readable format. This allows you to review what information we have collected, understand how it has been used, and verify its accuracy. You can access much of your information directly through your account settings at any time.

Right to Correction: You have the right to request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal information we hold about you. You can update most of your account information directly through your profile settings. For other corrections, you may submit a request to our privacy team, and we will address it promptly. We rely on accurate information to provide our Services effectively, and we encourage you to keep your information current and correct.

Right to Deletion: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions. You can delete your account at any time through your account settings, which will initiate the deletion of your personal data. Upon receiving a verified deletion request, we will delete your information from our active systems and direct our service providers to do the same, except where retention is required by law, necessary to complete transactions, or needed for legitimate business purposes as described in this policy. Please note that some information, such as anonymized analytics data or content shared publicly, may not be fully deletable.

Right to Data Portability: You have the right to request a copy of your personal information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, enabling you to transfer that data to another service provider if you choose. We support data portability and will provide your data in a format such as JSON or CSV upon request. You can request a data export through your account settings or by contacting our privacy team.

Right to Opt-Out: You have the right to opt out of certain data processing activities. Specifically, you can: opt out of receiving promotional communications by using the unsubscribe link in our emails or through your notification preferences; opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device advertising identifier settings or using platform-level opt-out mechanisms; opt out of location tracking by disabling location permissions in your device settings; and opt out of cookies and similar tracking technologies through your browser settings. Please note that opting out of certain data processing may affect the functionality and personalization of our Services.

Exercising Your Rights: To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@black94.app. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days, or within the timeframe required by applicable law. We may ask for information to verify your identity before processing your request. We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights.

7. Children's Privacy

The Black94 platform and Services are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age of digital consent in your jurisdiction, which may be 14, 15, or 16 depending on your location). We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose personal information from children under the age of 13. Our account registration process requires users to confirm that they are at least 13 years of age, and we use reasonable measures to verify the age of our users.

If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child under the age of 13 has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately at privacy@black94.app. Upon receiving verifiable notice that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, we will take prompt steps to delete that information from our servers and records. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete that information as quickly as commercially feasible.

We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable children's privacy laws. In jurisdictions where the minimum age for data processing consent is higher than 13 (such as 16 in certain European countries under the GDPR), we require that users meet the applicable age threshold. We encourage parents and guardians to actively participate in their children's online activities and to supervise their use of social media platforms and other online services. We provide additional safety resources and parental guidance features within our platform to support online safety for younger users.
